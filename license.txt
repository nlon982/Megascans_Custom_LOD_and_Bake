Megascans Custom LOD & Baking Tool

Description: 
This tool allows you to create a custom LOD and bake out maps for Quixel Megascans assets.
Using a Megascan Asset subnetwork imported from Quixel Bridge, you can generate a better looking LOD + bake out some additional texture maps.
